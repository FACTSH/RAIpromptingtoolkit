AAblation (Remove)

Remove words to test how much they actually matter to the model’s behavior.

What does it show?

We remove words (e.g., formal) and compare the outputs. Big changes mean the word carries real weight. Small changes suggest the model is ignoring it.

BCounterfactual (Replace)

Swap terms with “what-if” alternatives to see how the model’s tone or content shifts.

How do replacements work?

Each line is a pair like formal, informal. We replace those words in your prompt, rerun the model, then compare outputs and analysis.
